Ontology Specification

The Semantic Hadith Ontology: Description back to ToC

The SemanticHadith Ontology is a comprehensive knowledge representation framework designed to semantically model the rich corpus of Islamic Hadith literature. It provides a structured and interconnected schema for capturing the various components and relationships within Hadith texts, including narrators, chains of transmission, textual content, thematic topics, and contextual references. By leveraging Semantic Web technologies, the ontology facilitates semantic enrichment, interoperability, and knowledge discovery across diverse Hadith collections and repositories. With its modular design and adherence to best practices in ontology development, the SemanticHadith Ontology serves as a foundational resource for advancing research, analysis, and interpretation of Hadith literature within the Semantic Web ecosystem.
